What Antara does (in plain English)

Antara is built around a simple loop: stabilise state, make meaning, integrate and learn. You can come in through the body (breathwork, posture), through language (journalling), or through conversation (agentic prompts that help you stay honest without getting dramatic).

We keep the interface calm on purpose. Your brain already has enough tabs open.

Privacy, by default

Antara is designed to be privacy-first. We avoid collecting what we do not need, we keep analytics opt-in, and we aim for the least invasive path that still works.

If something needs a camera or microphone, the product should explain why, and still be useful if you say “no”.
