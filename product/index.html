Product
A calmer way to do wellbeing

Antara is an agentic AI, neuroadaptive emotional-wellbeing companion grounded in neuroscience, neuropsychology, and psychosomatic healing, built to help people regulate their nervous system. Antara detects signals the human eye often misses, including facial micro-expressions, posture, voice cues (pace, tone, calmness), patterns in word choice, and the emotional subtext people struggle to name, including stress responses shaped by past.

It does not manage symptoms. It helps regulate the nervous system, make sense of emotional signals, and reconnect mind and body

It doesn’t just tell you what to do. It explains why something helps (and why it sometimes doesn’t), using clear conversation, real-time behavioural and neruo-state signals, and a carefully designed agentic AI engine that keeps you honest without turning everything into drama delivering real therapeutic workflows.

The Antara loop

1) Stabilise state. Get safe in your body first so you can stay present without flooding or shutting down.

2) Make meaning. Small actions that close the loop, rather than “understanding” becoming a hobby.

3) Integrate parts. Let the body complete and release the stress pattern so the new response sticks next time.

Underneath, Antara can use posture signals, micro-expression cues, and voice markers to enrich insights. But the product should never force that. You stay in charge.

Designed for low cognitive load

Fewer screens, clearer choices, and a UI that doesn’t shout. We use gentle motion and haptics, but never in a way that blocks the flow.

Bonus: it also looks rather nice on OLED.

Modules
Reflect

Intelligent mirror that learns your inner structure (voice, words, context), then helps you name what’s happening and why.

Energy & Breath

Align your breath. Map your body’s emotion points. Release your energy. Not just what but understand why, science-led.

Elevate

Mindset and meaning training. Turn down threat-scanning, rewrite the default story, and take small steps towards what matters.

Posture

Body-aware posture cues, with optional camera-based analysis. Understand how your body behaves under certain stress.

Heal

Deep repair, body-first. Map sensations and symptoms, release stored stress, and rebuild safety through short, gentle practices.

Insights

You and Your patterns over time, not labels. Trends, triggers, neruo-chemical states, what happened, and crucially, “what helps”.
