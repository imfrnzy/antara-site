Product
A calmer way to do wellbeing

Antara is not a content library. It’s a system for state change and integration. The goal is fewer spirals, more agency, and a body that doesn’t feel like an enemy.

The Antara loop

1) Stabilise state. Breath, posture, sensory grounding. Not as a ritual. As a reset.

2) Make meaning. Journalling + guided reflection that helps you name what’s happening.

3) Integrate and learn. Small actions that close the loop, rather than “understanding” becoming a hobby.

Underneath, Antara can use posture signals, micro-expression cues, and voice markers to enrich insights. But the product should never force that. You stay in charge.

Designed for low cognitive load

Fewer screens, clearer choices, and a UI that doesn’t shout. We use gentle motion and haptics, but never in a way that blocks the flow.

Bonus: it also looks rather nice on OLED.

Modules
Reflect

An intelligent mirror that learns your inner structure (voice, words, micro-expressions, context), then helps you name what’s happening and why.

Connect

Breathwork rounds, voice guidance, and timed pacing for settling.

Elevate

Mindset and meaning training. Turn down threat-scanning, rewrite the default story, and take small steps towards what matters.

Heal

Deep repair, body-first. Map sensations and symptoms, release stored stress, and rebuild safety through short, gentle practices.

Posture

Body-aware posture cues, with optional camera-based analysis.

Insights

Patterns over time, not judgement. Trends, triggers, and “what helps”.
