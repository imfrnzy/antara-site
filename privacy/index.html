Privacy Policy

Effective: October 2025 • Last updated: October 2025

Antara (“we”, “us”) is a wellness and self-reflection app. We design Antara to be privacy-first and transparent.

Age Restriction

Antara is intended for adults aged 18+. If you are under 18, do not use the app.

Information We Collect

  • Provided by you: account email, profile details, journals/reflections, support messages.
  • With permission: camera/microphone for optional features; health & motion data if you connect supported sources.
  • Automatically: usage analytics, device/app diagnostics, crash reports.

How We Use Information

  • Provide wellness insights and personalisation.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the app.
  • Send essential communications and support responses.

Sharing

We share data only with service providers that operate our service (e.g., hosting, analytics, payments) under agreements limiting their use to providing services to us. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect safety and rights.

Data Storage & Security

Data is stored with reputable vendors (e.g., Supabase) with encryption in transit and at rest. No system is 100% secure; please protect your device and account.

Your Rights

You can access, edit, or delete your data in the app (Profile → Settings → Delete Account). Depending on your region (e.g., GDPR/CCPA), you may have additional rights to access, correct, delete, or port your data.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from those under 18. If you believe a minor has provided information, contact us and we will delete it.

Contact

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in the app or by email if provided.
