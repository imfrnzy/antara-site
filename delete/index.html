Account & Data Deletion

You can delete your Antara account and personal data at any time. This page explains the two supported ways to do it — in‑app or by email request — so you can still act even if you’ve uninstalled the app.

Option A — Delete in the app (recommended)

  1. Open Antara on your phone.
  2. Go to Menu ▸ Settings ▸ Account.
  3. Tap Delete account & data and confirm.

This immediately schedules your account for deletion. Most data is removed promptly; certain encrypted backups may persist for a short period to support secure recovery and legal compliance.

Option B — Request by email (if you can’t access the app)

Fill in the form below. Your mail app will open with a pre‑filled request to contact@antara.icu.

We may ask you to verify ownership of the account email. If you no longer have access to that email, state this and include any transaction IDs (Google Play) to help us verify your request.

What happens next

  • Confirmation: We’ll acknowledge your request by email.
  • Deletion: Once verified, we delete your account and associated personal data. Purchase records may be retained where required by law.
  • Backups: Encrypted backups are purged on their regular cycle.

For details, see our Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.
